SANKALP CAREER INSTITUTE — STUDENT POLICY DOCUMENT
(Sample / fictional document for testing. Not a real institute.)

=====================================================================
1. ADMISSION PROCESS
=====================================================================
Admission is confirmed only after the registration form is submitted
and the registration fee is paid. A student is considered enrolled once
a confirmation SMS and a fee receipt have been issued by the institute
office. Verbal confirmation, phone calls, or WhatsApp messages do not
count as confirmed admission.

=====================================================================
2. ELIGIBILITY
=====================================================================
The 2-year JEE and NEET programs are open to students who have passed
Class 10. The 1-year programs are open to students currently in Class 12
or who have completed Class 12. The Foundation program is open to
students in Class 9 and Class 10. There is no entrance test for
admission, but students must submit valid academic records.

=====================================================================
3. DOCUMENTS REQUIRED AT ADMISSION
=====================================================================
At the time of admission, the student must submit: a copy of the Class 10
marksheet, a copy of the most recent marksheet, two passport-size
photographs, a copy of the student's Aadhaar card, and a copy of one
parent's or guardian's Aadhaar card. Original documents are verified at
the office and returned the same day.

=====================================================================
4. FEE AND INSTALLMENTS
=====================================================================
The full course fee may be paid at once or in up to three installments.
If paying in installments, the first installment is due at admission, the
second within 60 days, and the third within 120 days. A delay of more
than 15 days on any installment may result in suspension of class access
until the due amount is cleared. The registration fee is a separate
one-time charge and is included in the first installment.

=====================================================================
5. REFUND POLICY
=====================================================================
Refunds are processed only on a written application submitted at the
institute office in the prescribed format, signed by a parent or guardian.
Refund requests made by email, phone, SMS, or WhatsApp are not accepted.
The registration fee is non-refundable under all circumstances.

If a student withdraws before classes begin, the full course fee is
refunded after deducting the registration fee and the cost of any study
material already issued.

If a student withdraws within 15 days of the start of classes, 75 percent
of the tuition fee is refunded, after deducting the registration fee and
study material cost.

If a student withdraws within 30 days of the start of classes, 50 percent
of the tuition fee is refunded.

After 30 days from the start of classes, no refund of the tuition fee is
provided.

Refunds are credited only to the bank account of the parent or guardian,
and a copy of a cancelled cheque must be submitted with the application.

=====================================================================
6. DISCIPLINE AND CONDUCT
=====================================================================
Students are expected to attend regularly and maintain at least 75 percent
attendance. Mobile phones are not permitted inside classrooms. A student
rusticated from the institute on disciplinary grounds is not eligible for
any refund.

=====================================================================
7. TRANSFER AND BATCH CHANGE
=====================================================================
A student may request a one-time batch change within the first 30 days,
subject to seat availability in the requested batch. Admissions are not
transferable to another student or to another study center.
